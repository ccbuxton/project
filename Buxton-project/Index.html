Our Mission

AggiePulse is dedicated to enhancing the student experience by fostering communication, collaboration, and engagement among student groups at NC A&T State University. Our platform serves as a hub where students can connect, participate in group activities, and stay informed about upcoming events, all while contributing to a thriving campus community.
